    Level A1

    The initial step of learning German is the A1 Level, which allows you to converse effortlessly about specific necessities such as introducing yourself, eating and drinking, buying, and living. Some extremely basic grammatical constructions can be used.

    The German A1 exam consists of a written and an oral part. The written exam is 65 minutes long. Without further preparation, you will usually undergo an oral exam afterwards directly.

    The A2 level of German is the second stage of learning German. You can easily converse and have short chats in ordinary settings. You can correctly employ simple grammatical constructions.
